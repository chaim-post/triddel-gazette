About

What is the Triddel Gazette?

The Triddel Gazette is the oldest newspaper of the city of Triddel, One of the central locations in my pathfinder roleplaying setting.

Who writes it?

I (Chaim Post) am a student living in the Pacific Northwest. I have been GM'ing off and on for family members and friends for about five years. This is my first attempt at a site for roleplaying and, at-least initially, it will be a place to post digital ‘handouts’ for my players.

Problems? Questions?
