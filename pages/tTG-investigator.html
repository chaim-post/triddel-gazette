In this online roleplaying adventure take control of the newest reporter for the Triddel Gazette. Joining you will be Rupert Torrens, a senior investigative reporter for the Gazette. He will assist you as you explore the city looking for the perfect first story to start your career.

You Find yourself in the Offices of the Triddel Gazette, the most widely distrubuted newspaper in Triddel. You were recently hired by the Gazette as it's newest investigative reporter. You have been told to meet Rupert Torrens, a senior reporter, who will be working with you during your first assignment. Looking around the large lobby full of red leather benches you see an old man speaking with the receptionist. The receptionist motions for him to sit down and he takes the spot on the bench next to you. As the old man takes his seat he says to you.

"Good day to you."

You return his greeting.

"Pardon me my questions but I like to know what sort of person I am speaking to and my eyes are not what they used to be, would you mind telling me what sort of person you are?"

You explain that you are a reporter and that you are waiting for your partner so that you can look for your first story.
